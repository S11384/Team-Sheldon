Welcome to TEAM Sheldon

We are proud participants of the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) from Sheldon College, Redlands. Our team is dedicated to innovation, teamwork, and excellence in robotics.

About Us

TEAM Sheldon is a group of passionate students from Sheldon College working together to solve real-world problems through robotics. We design, build, and program robots to compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge.

Our Achievements

  • 2023 - Queensland FTC Championship - Finalists
  • 2022 - Innovative Design Award
  • 2021 - Regional Qualifiers - Winners
